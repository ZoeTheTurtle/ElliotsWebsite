Katy Doyle, High School senior,
Issaquah High School, Bellevue college
Climate justice matters to me because I value stability in our earth for everyone. I can see how low income communities are severely impacted by the environment due to social inequalities. I love exploring the outdoors whether it's playing soccer, hiking, biking, skiing or surfing and I believe we need to make changes now so we can all continue to enjoy it. I understand the data behind climate change and feel a responsibility to support systemic solutions for a more just and sustainable future.
