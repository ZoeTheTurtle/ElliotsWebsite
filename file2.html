To solve climate injustice, we need systemic change. This means transitioning to renewable energy, and making sure policies, like climate reparations and just transitions, help those most impacted by climate change. On a smaller scale, we need to make sure we are recycling, consider switching to electric vehicles and consider switching our homes electricity to wind or solar power. It's about making sure everyone has a chance to thrive in a healthy, safe environment. Sociological understanding can guide us in addressing the root causes of inequality and ensuring fair solutions for the future. The need to integrate climate change across all fields especially schools, just like anti-racism, and other political topics. In addition government agencies like the FTC can also play a large role in the climate crisis. By restricting which products could be advertised as sustainable, and increase the punishment for companies that practice greenwashing.
