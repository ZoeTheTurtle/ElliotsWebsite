Climate justice in sociology connects climate change and environmental issues to social inequality. Social structures set up throughout the world have impacted the environment and different kinds of people all over the world. Climate Justice has everything to do with widespread personal lifestyle choices in addition to understanding individual responsibilities of voting and activism in order to produce change and ultimately preserve life on earth.
